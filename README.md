<h1> Promptly </h1>
<h3>Tagline: Your micro-reminder, instantly.</h3>
<p>This name and tagline are short, memorable, and directly communicate the app's core purpose. <br>"Promptly" implies both speed and immediacy, which are central to the app's value proposition.</p>
<h3>Problem Statement</h3>
<p>“People struggle to quickly capture and remember fleeting micro-tasks because traditional to-do apps are too slow and high-friction, often causing important thoughts to be forgotten.”</p>
